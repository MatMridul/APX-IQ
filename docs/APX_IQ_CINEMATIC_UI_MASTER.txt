4) MICRO-INTERACTION SYSTEM (F1-GRADE UX)
=====================================================================

4.1 Hover Interactions
- Panels emit faint gold edge glow.
- Metrics slightly increase brightness.
- Cursor proximity triggers micro pulse.

4.2 Click Interactions
- Gold ripple effect from click origin.
- Panel compresses by 2px and rebounds.
- Telemetry values momentarily intensify.

4.3 Data Update Interactions
- Updated values slide upward by 2px.
- Gold pulse indicates change.
- Graph points briefly glow.

4.4 Alert Interactions
- Red flash overlay (100ms).
- Panel vibration (1–2px jitter).
- Sound cue (optional future feature).

4.5 Focus Interactions
- Selected driver panel gains gold border.
- Other panels dim slightly.
